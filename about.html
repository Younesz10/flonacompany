About Us

Our Story

Established in 2015, Flona is a pioneering force in the household cleaning product industry. With a steadfast commitment to quality and innovation, we have rapidly grown to become a trusted name in Kurdistan, Iraq, and beyond.

Our Mission

At Flona, we are dedicated to simplifying and enhancing the lives of our customers through superior cleaning solutions. By consistently delivering products of the highest quality, we aim to foster cleanliness, comfort, and well-being in every home we touch.

Our Products

From dishwashing detergents to laundry essentials and an array of cleaning agents, Flona offers a comprehensive range of household cleaning products designed to tackle the toughest messes with ease. Crafted with precision and care, our products are manufactured in Turkey, utilizing cutting-edge technology and the finest ingredients to ensure unparalleled performance and safety.

Our Presence

With our main office located in Zakho and a secondary office in Duhok, both nestled in the heart of Kurdistan, Iraq, Flona proudly serves the local community and beyond. Our commitment to excellence has enabled us to expand our reach, and today, our products can be found in stores across Kurdistan, Iraq, bringing cleanliness and freshness to homes everywhere.

Our Approach

At Flona, we understand the importance of forging strong partnerships. As a business-to-business transactions company, we work closely with wholesalers, distributors, and retailers to ensure seamless access to our products. Through collaborative efforts, we strive to build lasting relationships based on trust, reliability, and mutual success.
